Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Erwin's Depot

General Questions

What makes Erwin's Depot different from other furniture retailers? ▼

At Erwin's Depot, we pride ourselves on offering quality furniture at affordable prices. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern design, ensuring every piece is built to last. Our extensive selection includes everything from classic to contemporary styles, and we offer customization options to match your unique taste and space requirements.

Do you offer custom furniture? ▼

Yes! We offer customization options for many of our furniture pieces. You can choose different fabrics, finishes, sizes, and configurations to create furniture that perfectly fits your home. Contact our design team to discuss your custom furniture needs. Custom orders typically take 14-28 business days to complete.

What materials do you use in your furniture? ▼

We use high-quality materials including solid hardwoods, engineered wood, premium upholstery fabrics, genuine and faux leather, and durable metal frames. All our materials meet industry standards for quality and safety. Product descriptions on our website provide detailed material information for each item.

Do you have showrooms where I can see furniture in person? ▼

Yes, we have showrooms in select locations. Visit our website's "Locations" page to find a showroom near you. Our showrooms feature a wide selection of our furniture where you can see, touch, and test items before purchasing. Our knowledgeable staff is available to answer questions and help you find the perfect pieces for your home.

Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take? ▼

Shipping times vary based on your location and the item. In-stock items typically ship within 1-3 business days and arrive in 5-7 business days via standard shipping. Made-to-order items take 7-14 business days to produce before shipping. Large furniture items are shipped via freight and may take 7-10 business days for delivery. You'll receive tracking information once your order ships.

Do you offer free shipping? ▼

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on orders over $1,000. For orders under $1,000, shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on the size, weight, and destination of your order. We also frequently run promotions with free or discounted shipping, so be sure to check our website or sign up for our newsletter.

What is White Glove Delivery? ▼

White Glove Delivery is our premium delivery service that includes scheduled delivery, professional assembly, placement in your room of choice, and removal of all packaging materials. This service is perfect for large items and ensures a hassle-free experience. It's available for an additional fee starting at $150.

Can I track my order? ▼

Absolutely! Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on the carrier's website or through your Erwin's Depot account dashboard. You'll also receive updates as your order progresses through the delivery process.

Do you ship internationally? ▼

Yes, we ship to many countries worldwide. International shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location. Please note that international customers are responsible for any customs duties, import taxes, or fees. Large furniture items may have restrictions or additional shipping requirements for certain countries.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy? ▼

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Furniture must be in original, unassembled condition (if shipped unassembled) with all parts and packaging. Custom and personalized items are final sale unless defective. Some items may be subject to restocking fees. For complete details, please visit our Returns & Refunds Policy page.

How do I initiate a return? ▼

Contact our customer service team at returns@erwinsdepot.com or call 1-800-ERWINS within 30 days of delivery. Provide your order number and reason for return. We'll send you a Return Authorization number and instructions. For large items, we'll arrange carrier pickup. For small items, we'll provide a prepaid shipping label.

Who pays for return shipping? ▼

If the return is due to our error (wrong item, defective, or damaged), we provide free return shipping. If you're returning for other reasons (change of mind, wrong size, etc.), you're responsible for return shipping costs. Return shipping for large furniture typically ranges from $75-$200 depending on size and location.

Can I exchange an item? ▼

Yes! We're happy to exchange items for a different size, color, or style. Follow our return process and indicate you'd like an exchange. We'll process your exchange once we receive and inspect the returned item. Exchanges are subject to product availability, and price differences will be refunded or charged accordingly.

Assembly & Care

Does furniture come assembled? ▼

It depends on the item. Some furniture arrives fully assembled, while others require assembly. Product descriptions on our website indicate whether assembly is required. All furniture that requires assembly comes with detailed instructions and necessary hardware. You can also add White Glove Delivery service for professional assembly.

How do I care for my furniture? ▼

Care instructions vary by material. Generally, dust regularly with a soft cloth, avoid direct sunlight and heat sources, and clean spills immediately. For wood furniture, use appropriate wood cleaner. For upholstered furniture, vacuum regularly and follow fabric care instructions. Detailed care instructions are included with your furniture and available on our website.

Do you offer assembly services? ▼

Yes! Our White Glove Delivery service includes professional assembly. Alternatively, you can hire local assembly services in your area. Many customers also successfully assemble their furniture using our detailed instructions. If you have questions during assembly, our customer service team is happy to help.

Payment & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and financing options through our partners. All payments are processed securely through encrypted connections to protect your financial information.

Do you offer financing options? ▼

Yes! We offer financing options through our partner lenders. You can apply for financing during checkout. Depending on your credit approval, you may qualify for special financing offers including 0% APR for qualified purchases. Terms and conditions apply.

Do you price match? ▼

We strive to offer competitive prices. While we don't have an official price match policy, we regularly review our pricing to ensure great value. If you find a lower price on an identical item, contact our customer service team and we'll do our best to work with you.
