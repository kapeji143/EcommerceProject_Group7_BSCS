Returns & Refunds Policy

Last updated: November 3, 2025

1. Return Policy Overview

At Erwin's Depot, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your furniture, you may return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Important: Custom-made and personalized furniture items are final sale and cannot be returned unless defective. Assembled furniture may be subject to a restocking fee.

2. Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your furniture must meet the following conditions:

  • Returned within 30 days of delivery
  • In original, unassembled condition (if shipped unassembled)
  • In original packaging or adequately protected for return shipment
  • Not a custom or personalized item
  • Free from damage, stains, or excessive wear
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase (order number or receipt)

3. How to Initiate a Return

Follow these simple steps to return your furniture:

  1. Contact Us: Email returns@erwinsdepot.com or call 1-800-ERWINS within 30 days of delivery
  2. Provide Information: Include your order number, reason for return, and photos if applicable
  3. Receive Authorization: We'll send you a Return Authorization (RA) number and return instructions
  4. Schedule Pickup: For large items, we'll arrange carrier pickup. For small items, use the prepaid label provided
  5. Prepare Item: Disassemble if necessary and pack securely with the RA number visible

4. Return Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs vary based on the reason for return:

Free Return Shipping

  • Defective or damaged items
  • Wrong item shipped
  • Quality issues verified by our team

Customer-Paid Return Shipping

  • Change of mind
  • Ordered wrong size, color, or style
  • No longer needed

For customer-paid returns of large furniture items, return shipping typically ranges from $75-$200 depending on size and location. Small items can be returned via standard shipping. We recommend using a trackable shipping service and purchasing shipping insurance.

5. Restocking Fees

Restocking fees may apply in certain situations:

  • Assembled Furniture: 15% restocking fee if item was delivered assembled and is being returned for non-defect reasons
  • Large Items: 10% restocking fee for items over $500 (non-defect returns)
  • No Restocking Fee: Defective items, damaged items, or our shipping errors

6. Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your returned furniture:

  • Inspection: 3-5 business days after receipt
  • Refund Approval: Email notification sent within 24 hours of inspection
  • Refund Issued: 7-10 business days to original payment method

Original shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective product. Any applicable restocking fees will be deducted from your refund.

Note: Depending on your bank or credit card company, it may take an additional 2-5 business days for the refund to appear in your account.

7. Exchanges

We're happy to exchange your furniture for a different size, color, or style. To request an exchange:

  1. Follow the return process outlined above
  2. Indicate in your return request that you'd like an exchange
  3. Specify the replacement item details
  4. We'll ship your exchange item once we receive and inspect your return

If there's a price difference, we'll either refund or charge the difference accordingly. Exchanges are subject to product availability.

8. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive damaged or defective furniture:

  • Notify us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damage or defect from multiple angles
  • Note any damage on the delivery receipt if possible
  • Keep all packaging materials

We will provide prepaid return shipping and either send a replacement or issue a full refund, including original shipping costs and any assembly fees if applicable.

9. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Custom-made or personalized furniture
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Clearance"
  • Mattresses and bedding (for hygiene reasons)
  • Furniture that has been modified or altered
  • Items with visible wear, stains, or damage caused by customer
  • Items without proof of purchase
  • Returns initiated after 30 days from delivery

10. International Returns

International customers are responsible for return shipping costs and any customs fees. Please contact us before returning an international order to receive specific instructions. International returns may take longer to process.

11. Contact Us

For questions about returns or to initiate a return, please contact us at returns@erwinsdepot.com or call 1-800-ERWINS. Our customer service team is available Monday-Friday, 9am-6pm EST.
