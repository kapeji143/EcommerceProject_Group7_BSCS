Shipping Policy

Last updated: November 3, 2025

1. Processing Time

All orders at Erwin's Depot are carefully prepared and quality-checked before shipping. Processing times vary depending on the product and current order volume:

  • In-Stock Items: 1-3 business days
  • Made-to-Order Furniture: 7-14 business days
  • Custom Furniture: 14-28 business days
  • Large Orders (5+ items): 5-10 business days

You will receive an email notification when your order has been processed and shipped.

2. Shipping Methods

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs:

Domestic Shipping (within the country)

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days ($25.00 - Small items)
  • Standard Shipping: 7-10 business days ($75.00 - Large furniture)
  • White Glove Delivery: Scheduled delivery with assembly ($150.00+)
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days (Available for small items only - $50.00)

International Shipping

  • International Standard: 15-30 business days (rates vary by location)
  • International Express: 7-14 business days (rates vary by location)

Free Shipping: Orders over $1,000 qualify for free standard domestic shipping!

3. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on:

  • Order weight and dimensions
  • Delivery destination
  • Selected shipping method
  • Special handling requirements (stairs, elevator, etc.)

Final shipping costs will be displayed at checkout before you complete your purchase.

4. White Glove Delivery Service

Our premium White Glove Delivery service includes:

  • Scheduled delivery appointment
  • Professional assembly and setup
  • Placement in room of choice
  • Removal of all packaging materials
  • Basic furniture inspection

This service is highly recommended for large furniture items and is available for an additional fee starting at $150.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your package by:

  • Clicking the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
  • Visiting the carrier's website directly
  • Logging into your Erwin's Depot account and viewing order status

6. Delivery Issues

We work with trusted shipping partners to ensure safe delivery. However, if issues arise:

Lost or Stolen Packages

If your package is marked as delivered but you haven't received it, please contact us within 48 hours. We will work with the carrier to investigate and resolve the issue.

Damaged Items

If your furniture arrives damaged, please:

  • Take photos of the damaged item and packaging
  • Note any damage on the delivery receipt if possible
  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Keep all packaging materials for carrier inspection

We will arrange for a replacement or full refund for damaged items.

7. Address Changes

Please ensure your shipping address is accurate before completing your order. If you need to change your shipping address:

  • Before shipping: Contact us immediately at support@erwinsdepot.com
  • After shipping: You may be able to redirect your package through the carrier's website

Address changes after shipping may incur additional fees.

8. International Orders

International customers are responsible for:

  • Customs duties and import taxes
  • Compliance with local import regulations
  • Any additional fees imposed by customs

These fees are not included in our shipping costs and must be paid by the recipient upon delivery. Please note that large furniture items may have shipping restrictions to certain countries.

9. Contact Us

For questions about shipping or to track your order, please contact us at shipping@erwinsdepot.com or call our customer service team at 1-800-ERWINS.
